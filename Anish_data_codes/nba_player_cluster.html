NBA Player Cluster

It's always interesting to see a grouping of any entity through the secondary data. Here we have identified three distinct group of NBA players who are playing in the 2018-19 season, based on their latest 10 years of history. We restricted our analysis to the players who have more than 50 NBA games under their belt.


The size of the circles in the scatter plot below indicates the annual salary (2018-19) of each player, whereas the colors represent individual cluster. The Gantt chart at the bottom represent the distribution of each statistics with each cluster to give a sense of what each cluster represents. You can use the interaction among the charts to focus into one specific cluster.


Source: All data was scraped from https://stats.nba.com
