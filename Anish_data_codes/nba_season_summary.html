NBA Team Strategy Trend in the Last Ten Years

NBA has evolved over the last 10 years and is very much a different game than it was just a decade ago. The rules of the game are mostly the same, so are the teams. Looking at the summary statistics from the last 10 NBA seasons we can seen a huge shift in the way the game is being played.


Below we see a trend of 12 key basket ball statistics trending over the last 10 seasons. It is interesting to observe that how the teams have changed their focus from easier shots from closer to the basket to three-point shots. The overall points has increased consistantly over the last 10 years signifying how the sport is evolving in terms of speed and agility. We can expect some mind vlowing statistics in the future but while that happens it would be important to keep in mind that the players from different eras can not be compared based on their raw statistics.
