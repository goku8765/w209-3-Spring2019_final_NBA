Top30 All-time Players by Five Major Attributes

It is an interesting topic to explore all-time best NBA players from various perspectives. The most important measures of a player's performance are scores, rebounds, assists, blocks, and steals. Scoring is a among these measures because different scoring types, i.e, 2 pointer, 3 pointer, and free throws, reflect the player's power and strength on the court.


Regarding other measures, an excellent rebounding player can be either a good assister or a good blocker depending on his position. Another interesting sub-topic is that an assist expert is normally a steal expert, too. Thus, in the interactive visualizaion, users will see:


  • Top 30 scoring players with their scoring types
  • Top 30 rebounding players with theirs assists and blocks
  • Top 30 assisting players with their steals
  • Top 30 players for blocks and steals

    • Don't forget that some superstars are all-around players! Users can search using the dropdown menu to find out each top player's top records. A few of them appear on 3+ list!!!
