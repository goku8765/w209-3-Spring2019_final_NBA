NBA Twitter Influence

Twitter and other social media platforms has a great influence on the society. NBA players are no exeptions.


We have scraped about a million tweets from 379 NBA player's official twitter account and conducted sentiment analysis on each of them tweet

  • Only 23% of the activity from these players were retweets
  • More than half the tweets originated from an iPhone app for Twitter
  • An average length of individual tweet is 85 characters

    • As a next step we would like to anlyse these tweets in more detail to gain some predictive insights. But as of now the stage is open to users to play around and identify trends of their own interset.
