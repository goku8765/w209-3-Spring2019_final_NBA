Elo Ratings of All NBA Historical and Current Teams

Which team is better: the current Warriors or Jordan's Bulls? Are the Celtics the greatest franchise in NBA history? What about the Lakers? As long as there are fans following the NBA, debates like these will always happen. So next time you want to argue about your favorite team, use our NBA Explorer to find supporting evidence so you can defend even your hottest of takes.

If you want to compare the strengths of different teams, explore the history of the NBA by exploring the Elo ratings of every team in NBA history. Elo ratings are a method for quantifying the strength of a team based on game by game results. The Elo formula usees game score, location, date, and opponent strength to rate teams. This vizualization is inspired by fivethirtyeight.com's "The Complete History Of The NBA." To begin exploring:
  • Hover over a line to highlight that team
  • Search for your favorite team
  • Click on a line to see the Elo ratings for each game of that season
  • Hover over a line in the Single Season graph to see the result of each game and how it affected the team's Elo rating
